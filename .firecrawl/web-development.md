## Questions about web development we get asked a lot

What is the catch with a monthly website subscription?

No catch. You get a professionally built, conversion-focused website on a proven framework, customised for your brand, plus fully managed hosting, security, software updates, and support for one predictable monthly fee. No surprise invoices. Pricing is not published on the site — we discuss it live in a strategy call so we can give you an accurate, relevant number.

Do I own the website if I stop the subscription?

This is a managed service model, not a traditional build-and-hand-over arrangement. The site as hosted and managed by Digital Hall of Fame is no longer active under the subscription if you cancel. Buyout or migration is discussed at that point. We are transparent about this upfront — clients who understand the model from day one are the clients who get results.

Can I get a fully custom design or complex bespoke functionality?

The subscription uses proven, high-converting frameworks customised with your branding, content, and structure. Extensive bespoke development outside this scope is quoted separately as a project. For most established NZ businesses, a well-executed proven framework outperforms a bespoke build at twice the cost — because the strategy and execution matters far more than the platform.

Is this better than just building it myself on Wix or Squarespace?

With Wix you get a template and an afternoon of frustration. With us you get conversion strategy, professional setup, SEO-readiness from day one, ongoing technical management, security hardening, and a team invested in your results. The platform is the least important part. Strategy and execution are everything.

How long does it take to get a new site live?

Typically 2 to 4 weeks from strategy kick-off to launch, depending on content availability and approval speed. We move fast and set clear milestones from day one. You will always know where things stand.
