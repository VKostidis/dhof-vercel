FAQ

Questions we get a lot

Is it worth it to hire a digital marketing agency?
Yes, but only the right one. The wrong agency burns your budget and delivers PDFs full of vanity metrics. The right one builds a predictable profit engine. At DHOF, we only take on clients we genuinely believe we can deliver a disgusting return for. If the numbers won't work for your business, we'll tell you in the strategy call, before you spend a dollar.
How much does it cost to hire a digital marketing agency?
It depends entirely on your goals, industry, and competitive landscape, which is why we don't publish pricing. What we can tell you is that our positioning is accessible-premium: serious enough to filter tyre-kickers, accessible enough for an established NZ SME. Book a free strategy call and we'll give you a straight quote based on your actual scope.
How much does a digital marketer make in NZ?
A competent in-house digital marketer in NZ typically earns $65,000–$100,000+ per year, before tools, training, management overhead, and the risk of one person's skill gaps costing you market share. Partnering with a specialist agency gives you a full team's expertise, AI-integrated tools, and battle-tested strategy, often for less than a single salary.
What is the 3-3-3 rule in marketing?
The 3-3-3 rule is a messaging framework: you have 3 seconds to grab attention, 3 seconds to communicate your core value, and 3 minutes to convert interest into action. At DHOF, this principle shapes every ad, landing page, and piece of content we build, because if you haven't earned attention in the first three seconds, nothing else matters.
What digital marketing services does Digital Hall of Fame offer?
SEO, Google Ads (SEM/PPC), web development, social media advertising, video marketing, and AI consultancy. We're a full-service NZ digital marketing agency, and each service is delivered with singular focus on measurable ROI. No fluff, no filler, no BS.
How do I know if my current digital marketing is working?
If you can't directly link your marketing spend to leads, sales, or revenue growth, it's probably not working. The telltale signs: high ad spend with low conversions, Google rankings stuck on page 2+, a website that looks fine but generates nothing. Book a strategy call and we'll run a forensic audit on what's actually happening under the hood.
Do you work with businesses outside Auckland?
Yes. We're NZ-based and serve clients across the country, and internationally. Digital marketing doesn't care about geography. If you're an established business serious about growth, we can fight for you regardless of where in NZ (or the world) you operate.
How long before I see results from digital marketing?
Depends on the channel. Google Ads can drive traffic within hours of launch. SEO is a longer game, meaningful movement in 3–6 months, significant results at 6–12+ months. Web development improvements can lift conversion rates almost immediately. We set honest expectations from day one, and we report on real metrics, not vanity numbers.
